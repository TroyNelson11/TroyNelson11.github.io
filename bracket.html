Is this a part of the competition?

No, This is not a part of the competition. The bracket is meant to help you predict how many games a team might win / play this postseason. Simply click on the team you think will win the game.

The minus or plus number is the point spread for the home team. The over / under is the number of points expected in that game. At the end, counting the number of times you see the logo will help tell you how many games that team played.
